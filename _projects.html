www.localtakesf.com

Local Take is a San Francisco boutique featuring locally made items from artists in the Bay Area. They needed a nice clean landing page with a backend to support both artists and owners. The backend system helps users track inventory - in the store for owners and what has sold for the artists.

www.bresbo.com

Bresbo - a small Swedish startup needed a simple brochure site to get rolling. Driven by the mobile-first design, the site is built with HTML5/CSS3 responsive templates and includes both English and Swedish content translations.

missionartistsunited.org

Mission Artists United is a site used to promote artists in the Mission district of San Francisco. The site manages over 600 active artists providing them with a way to share their works online. The site includes user management, an administrator CMS for content delivery, MailChimp integration and runs on Ruby on Rails.

selectorschoice.bunnymatic.com

Selectors Choice showcases a couple local DJ's and their recorded sets. Admin users can upload tracks with tracklists, mp3s and other data. I designed and built the site - honing my CSS3 skills and learning about HTML5 audio tags and inline media players. Runs on Ruby on Rails. Hosted on Heroku.

bedsider.org

Bedsider is an online birth control support network for women 18-29 operated by The National Campaign to Prevent Teen and Unplanned Pregnancy, a private non-profit organization. It includes a custom CMS, integrated SMS and Email systems and runs on Ruby on Rails.

romeydesigns.com

This site highlights the work by San Francisco designer Jennifer Meyer. It includes a custom CMS and runs on Ruby with Sinatra.

ansonmills.com

Anson Mills strives to provide the best handmade mill goods from heirloom grains. Their business focuses on bringing back the Carolina Rice Kitchen food ways - breeding and growing grains for flavor, not for shelf life or visual appeal. This site serves to showcase their grain products, along with recipes based around their products. A full-featured CMS allows the company to update products, recipes and newsletters. Visitors can use the site to read recipes and order products. It is integrated with Shopify and includes a custom CMS. Built with Ruby on Rails.
