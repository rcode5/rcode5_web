missionartistsunited.org

Mission Artists United is a site used to promote artists in the Mission district of San Francisco. The site manages over 600 active artists providing them with a way to share their works online. The site includes user management, an administrator CMS for content delivery, MailChimp integration and runs on Ruby on Rails.

bedsider.org

Bedsider is an online birth control support network for women 18-29 operated by The National Campaign to Prevent Teen and Unplanned Pregnancy, a private non-profit organization. It includes a custom CMS, integrated SMS and Email systems and runs on Ruby on Rails.

romeydesigns.com

This site highlights the work by San Francisco designer Jennifer Meyer. It includes a custom CMS and runs on Ruby with Sinatra.

ansonmills.com

Anson Mills strives to provide the best handmade mill goods from heirloom grains. Their business focuses on bringing back the Carolina Rice Kitchen food ways - breeding and growing grains for flavor, not for shelf life or visual appeal. This site serves to showcase their grain products, along with recipes based around their products.
