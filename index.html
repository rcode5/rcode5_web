about

I've been working in software development for 20 years. After graduate school where I focused on image compression algorithm development, I worked for various science and techology companies, including 2rye, Carbon Five, Xmarks, BEA Systems, Symyx Technologies, and FAST Search. On the side, he helps run Mission Artists United an artists driven community which supports and promotes artists in the Mission District of the San Francisco.

I've contributed to the open source community with a couple of ruby gems (qr code generation in ruby, rosie, a sql/asset backup system), and by adding bits of code to several external open source projects.

I'm available for contract web application work. Though I typically lean towards ruby based projects, I've had experience in many other languages and am willing to entertain most any project. Please drop me a line at and let's see if I can help you out with your next project. jon at rcode5 dot com

missionartistsunited.org

Mission Artists United is a site used to promote artists in the Mission district of San Francisco. The site manages over 600 active artists providing them with a way to share their works online. The site includes user management, an administrator CMS for content delivery, MailChimp integration and runs on Ruby on Rails.

bedsider.org

Bedsider is an online birth control support network for women 18-29 operated by The National Campaign to Prevent Teen and Unplanned Pregnancy, a private non-profit organization. It includes a custom CMS, integrated SMS and Email systems and runs on Ruby on Rails.

romeydesigns.com

This site highlights the work by San Francisco designer Jennifer Meyer. It includes a custom CMS and runs on Ruby with Sinatra.
